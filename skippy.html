Skippy! Explore everyday!

Link to game page

Designer, Writer, Developer, and Artist.
Done in Unity
Winner of best art of Virtual Pet Jam in 2022

‘I am cool and friendly, but I need a little help…. I’m here to learn about the world. But exploring is turning out a little bit harder than I thought…. Please help me! I need food and somewhere to sleep. But most importantly, I need cough drops! To go on adventures! Let’s explore everyday!'

Skippy is a short FMV (Full Motion Video) game made for the Virtual Pet Jam 2022 in just over 2 weeks. It combines digitized images, live-action video, and doodle-like drawings for its graphics, and is a simple gameplay-light experience, and is inspired by things like Moon anti-rpg and Petscop.

As part of team of 4 people, I took on multiple roles for this short project. I programmed, did the writing, and most of the art of the game, including making the skippy model out of clay, and taking it around and filming all of the shots. It was really rewarding to play around with a more narrative-based game, and one where I had more creative control over.
