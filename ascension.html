Ascension

Link to game page


Programmer and Designer
Done in Unity

Play as a cute space explorer in a zero-gravity space station! Use your ray gun to ascend through the twists and turns and knock evil aliens out of your way! Make your way through levels by using your weapon to both propel and protect yourself. Done as part of a team for the Brown/RISD game development club in the spring semester of 2022.

I helped work on programming, designing the gameplay mechanics, and was the lead level designer. This was my first game with 3D levels, which was very different from what I had done before, both in terms of design and programming. It was a great challenge, and made me excited to play around more with level design.
