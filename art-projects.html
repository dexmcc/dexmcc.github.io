Artist Statement

How big is the rain? Dexter McChesney has based a practice around finding questions like these and figuring out how we answer them. exploring the systems and narratives we create to comprehend the world around us, Dexter focuses on using glass’ boundary-defying nature as a way of delving into what separates man and nature. Dexter received a BFA in glass from the Rhode Island School of Design, and a BA in modern culture and media from Brown University.
