What makes a map a map? In Mappings, I aimed to create an object that contains a pattern that resembles a topographical form without any labels as to indicate whether or not it is a 'mapping' of a real place or thing. By drawing the viewer into a space contained within a tiny block, I hope to draw attention to the more abstract forms of mapping, and the beauty that can come from them.
