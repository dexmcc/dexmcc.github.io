During my time at Haystack, I was completely enamoured by how much moss covered the ground, especially on top of the rocks. I noticed the bald patches which hadn’t been covered yet, or had been ripped off, and wondered. Sometimes, I feel self conscious when I don't have a clothing item that I feel is as nice as the rest of my outfit, or feel exposed when I try out a new look, what if the rocks feel the same way? I know they are beautiful as they are, but I understand that feeling well, so I decided to try and make a toupee out of glass, that would cover these empty spots, while still showing off the beauty of the rock. I was thinking a lot about my own ability to show myself as a queer person, as well as the problems with self-image that are pushed so heavily onto trans people, and the ways we can overcome these spirals of self hate while not rejecting ourselves.
