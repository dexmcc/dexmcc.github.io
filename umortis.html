Umortis

Link to game page


Designer and Developer, programmed in Godot

Overall Winner of Game-like Jam 3: Puyo Puyo

What if Puyo-Puyo was a roguelike, but with meat!
Crush blobs of the four humours: blood, phlegm, black and yellow bile, to defeat all who stand before you in Umortis - a Puyo-Puyo-like that adds roguelike elements to the classic puzzle-arcade game. Rather than simply choosing how to attack or defend, your actions are controlled by what humours you crush, and are amplified by how well you can combo them. Find powerful items that change the way you approach playing the game, and see how far you are able to go, all while enjoying the gore-y artstyle that you wouldn't usually see in a game like this! Umortis was made in a month by a small team for the game-like jam in May 2025

I took on a large portion of the programming and design work for the game, being the largest project I had worked on to date. While reinforcing my skills in godot, I learned a lot about proper implementation of complex RPG and roguelike systems, as well as how to balance and design mechanics within roguelikes

Design

Beyond practicing my development skills, I had to learn how to properly design and balance systems that utilize a lot of different abilities. It was a very fulfilling challenge to try and come up with creative ways items and spells could affect the game, while making sure they were balanced. I worked hard to try and be as organized as I could be while brainstorming. I created a spreadsheet system to categorize things like enemies and spells once I came up with them, and used mind maps as a method to explore every possibility that could generate ideas for new ones, and used these to help structure how I would then design the code for the systems of the game before I started implementing them.
